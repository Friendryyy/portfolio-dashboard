## 🚀 5 Structural Catalysts (2026–2028)

### 1. Golden Dome / Defense Spending
- **DoD Space Budget FY2026:** $57.7B — all-time high
- SDA ($816M RKLB contract) + HASTE ($190M RKLB) + Anduril partnership
- Space Force spending could hit $40B+ in 2026 standalone
- **Winners:** RKLB (defense moat confirmed), LUNR (NASA contracts)

### 2. FCC Spectrum Modernization
- FCC rule overhaul: 8 satellites per region (ขยายจาก 1)
- Direct-to-device cellular service unlocked → ASTS primary beneficiary
- Also benefits: RKLB (Electron deployment capacity), FLY
- **Timeline:** Rules effective Q2 2026

### 3. SpaceX IPO Anticipation
- Valuation estimate: $1.75–2T (higher than Saudi Aramco at IPO)
- Sector halo effect: ทุกหุ้น space ได้รับ multiple re-rating
- **Risk:** IPO could suck capital FROM listed space stocks

### 4. NASA Artemis II
- Crewed lunar mission — demand for launch services + components
- **Winners:** Launch providers broadly

### 5. Executive Order — Space Commercial Expansion
- Trump administration favorable to commercial space
- Deregulation reduces barriers for new entrants + incumbent expansion
