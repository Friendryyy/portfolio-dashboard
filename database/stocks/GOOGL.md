### ⚖️ Antitrust — Complete Legal Picture

**Search Monopoly Case (DOJ + 35 State AGs):**
- Judge Mehta original ruling: Google illegal monopoly in search
- DOJ + 35 AGs **appealing** — seeking structural remedies:
  - **Chrome divestiture** (most severe option)
  - Mandatory choice screens for default search engine
  - Behavioral remedies (data sharing, API access)
- Morgan Stanley estimated impact: **$15–25B/year** revenue loss (choice screen alone = -8% ad revenue)
- Timeline: ruling expected **late 2026** — binary event

**AdTech Case:**
- Separate DOJ case: Google's AdX network antitrust
- Verdict: still pending
- Risk: forced divestiture of AdX (DoubleClick business)

**Probability Assessment (from notebook research):**
- Full structural breakup (Chrome divestiture): LOW probability (courts typically prefer behavioral)
- Choice screens (mandatory): MEDIUM-HIGH probability
- AdX divestiture: MEDIUM probability
- Stock impact of worst case: -25-30% (extreme scenario)
- **Base case: behavioral remedies only** = limited financial impact
