- **65% of all new US obesity prescriptions** go to Wegovy brand

**Wegovy for MASH (Liver Fibrosis) — FDA Approved:**
- MASH = Metabolic Dysfunction-Associated Steatohepatitis
- **First GLP-1 approved for liver fibrosis**
- Massively expands TAM: MASH affects 18M Americans
- Opens entirely new treatment category beyond obesity/diabetes

**CagriSema — The Major Failure:**
- Designed as "Next-Gen GLP-1" combining semaglutide + cagrilintide
- Phase 3 trial result (February 2026): **NON-INFERIORITY FAILURE vs Zepbound (LLY)**
- Zepbound: -50 lbs weight loss | CagriSema: -33 lbs weight loss
- Stock impact: **-16% in one day** (February 2026)
- FDA decision still pending late 2026 (long-shot approval possible)

**Amycretin — Last Major Pipeline Hope:**
- Dual GLP-1/Amylin analogue (new mechanism)
- Phase 3 trials (oral + injectable forms)
- Potential: **>20% weight loss** (superior to current Wegovy)
- Phase 3 interim data: expected 2027
- If successful = would reclaim leadership vs Zepbound

**Ozempic — Legacy Position:**
- Generic competition: China, Brazil, Canada (some markets already have generics)
- US patent protection until 2031
- Still core revenue driver despite market share pressure from Zepbound
